a - how many bytes the next record is offset by
b - 01
c - some sort of record number.  When it's unique, it's 2 bytes.  When it's not (i.e. 31), it's 4 bytes
d - 00 00 00 00 00 80
e - Class ID number, e.g. Warrior is #1, thus 01 00
f - null terminated string of class name.  Can have garbage after null since it's fixed width
g - exp % base
h - combat level
i - minimum hp per level
j - maximum hp per levelMagicLevel
k - weapons useable
        08 - all weapons
        04 - 1-handed weapons
        09 - staff
        07 - any blunt
l - armor useable
        09 - platemail
        06 - leather
        01 - silk
        07 - chainmail
        02 - ninja
        08 - scalemail
m - magic level
        00 - none
        01, 02, 03 - 1, 2, or 3
n - magic type
        00 - none
        01 - Mage
        02 - Priest
        03 - Druid
        04 - Bard
        05 - Kai
o through v are the codes
        1f - bash
        67 - class stealth
        23 - jumpkick
        22 - dodge
        26 - tracking
        8e - hit magical
        33 - anti-magic
        27 - thievery
        1d - kick or punch
        1e - kick or punch
        25 - picklocks or traps
        28 - picklocks or traps
        3a - crit
        3b is some error with the druid!  Quest/Class weapon or whatever
w through 4 are the values
